# IT Security Policy — Remote Access Procedures

This document outlines approved procedures for remote access to company systems.

## 1. VPN Requirements

All employees accessing internal systems remotely must use the company-approved VPN client. The VPN must be active before connecting to any internal resource.

## 2. Authentication Standards

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all remote access sessions. Acceptable second factors include hardware tokens and authenticator apps. SMS-based MFA is not permitted for systems classified as Confidential or higher.

## 3. Approved Devices

Only managed devices enrolled in the company MDM solution may be used for remote access. Personal devices are not permitted.

## 4. Session Management

Remote sessions must not exceed 8 hours. Idle sessions will be terminated after 30 minutes. Users must not share session credentials.

## 5. Incident Reporting

Any suspected security incident must be reported to the security team within 1 hour of discovery.
